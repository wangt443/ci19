How to: Curate a Playlist on Spotify

With a Spotify Playlist, you can...

  • listen.
  • play.
  • pause.
  • skip.
  • repeat.
  • like.
  • dislike.
  • share songs.
  • SHUFFLE.

Press "A" and "C" to change colors.
Press "B" to switch to night mode and to start song.

1. Press Search Button.
2. Type in song.
3. Press PLAY and listen to song.
4. Press "..."
5. Press "Add to Playlist."
6. Press "New Playlist."
Type in name of new playlist and press CREATE.
